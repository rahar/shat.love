1. Information We Collect

  • Chat Content: Messages and descriptions you provide to the chatbot, including conversations exchanged through our service.
  • Account Information (if applicable): Email address, username, or other identifiers you provide when creating an account.
  • Technical Data: Device type, operating system, app version, IP address, and usage statistics (e.g., frequency of use, feature engagement).
  • Support Requests: Information you provide when you contact us for assistance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver our chat-connection service and transmit messages between users.
  • Maintain chat history so that you can review past conversations.
  • Improve and personalize our Services, including troubleshooting, analytics, and product development.
  • Respond to user inquiries and support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Data Retention

We retain your chat history and other personal data for as long as you maintain an account or use our Services. You may request deletion of your chat history or account at any time (see Your Rights).

Right to Erasure: You can request removal of your chat history at any time. We will process verified requests as required by applicable law.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share information under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Vendors who help operate our Services (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) under contracts that protect your data.
  • Legal Compliance and Safety: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety or that of others.
  • Business Transfers: If we undergo a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred to a successor.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Subject to local law, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion of your chat history or account.
  • Objection to certain processing where legally permissible.

To exercise these rights, contact us at drinks.today@itsok.email. We will respond to verified requests within applicable legal timeframes.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16 (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have done so, please contact us to request deletion.

8. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, where data protection laws may differ. By using our Services, you consent to such transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the Last Updated date above and, when appropriate, provide in‑app notifications.
